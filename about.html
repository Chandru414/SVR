Shree Venkateshwara Rubbers

Manufacturers, and suppliers of various kinds of industrial rubber products since 1990.

We are one of the leading quality extruded rubber profiles, specialized in EPDM rubber profiles by holding experience of 30 years, since 1990, by manufacturing, and supplying of various kinds of rubber molded products, industrial extrusion profiles and rubber products. With the help of necessary infrastructure and skilled labors lead by professional, our company is producing custom and quality extrusion EPDM rubber products, which is cost effective.We are approved RATE CONTRACT by the Association of State Road Transport Undertaking(ASRTU), New Dehli for EPDM rubber glazings for Bus body section.We are approved vendors of KSRTC, BMTC, APSRTC, TSRTC, TNSTC.If the company has to sustain for long duration, the company has to full fill the expectations and need of the customer, because of duly satisfied customer we are here, by completing successful journey of 30 years in rubber manufacturing.
